Goals for: Green to Red | Red to Purple | Purple to Blue | Blue to Gold

Green to Red

  1. Learn one or two launchers & one follow up combo ✅
    • f,n,df,2 / f,F,3
    • df,3 ➔ df,1,2 ➔ f,n,df,3
  2. Don't Panic RA ✅
  3. Don't mash on knockdowns ✅
    • Wake up backwards: b
    • Spring kick: 3+4
    • Side Ukemi Right (during fall): 3
    • Side Ukemi Left (during fall): 4
  4. Backdash intentionally ✅
  5. Watch high-ranked replays ✅
    • SST to f,1+2 / EWGS
    • When low HP, think low parry!
  6. Use Frametraps
    • Mental Frametrap: Back Fist's 4 variations
    • Objective Frametrap: Back Fist ➔ Abolishing Fist ➔ Elusive Demon
  7. Practice Block Punishment drills ✅

Red to Purple

More Damage

  1. Better follow up: Level 2 (54 to 63 dmg) ✅
  2. Leverage Heat better than just Heat Smash:
    • Devil Impact to WGS: db,3+4,DF,2
    • Abyss Damnation: f,n,df,4,1,1
    • Devil Bleak: 1,1,2,F
    • Tempest Blaster: f,n,df,3

More Tekken

  1. Learn KBD
  2. Learn Wave Dash
  3. Utilize Frame Traps:
    • Stature Smash ➔ Abolishing Fist x2
    • 1,2,4,3 ➔ Soul Thrust
