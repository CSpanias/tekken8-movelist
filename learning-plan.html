General Rank-Based Goals

Green / Yellow / Orange
  • Choose main character, learn what buttons do
  • Movement comfort
  • Intentional input, i.e. no mashing
  • One basic frame trap
  • Watch replays to pick up moves
 Red
  • Frame-based movements
  • Punishments
  • Heat optimization
  • Sidestepping, Korean Back Dash, Wave Dash
 Purple
  • Matchup knowledge
  • Replay analysis
  • Combo optimization
  • Duck and punish highs in strings
  • Throw breaks
 Blue
  • Recognize opponent's patterns
  • In-depth matchup knowledge
  • Timing variation
 Tekken King+
  • Address individual weaknesses

Green, Yellow, and Orange

Movement comfort
  • Launcher(s) ➔ practice launchers
    • f,n,df,2
    • df,1,2
    • f,F,3
  • Follow Up Combo: df,3 + df,1,2 + f,n,df,3
  • Distance issues with launchers and Hell Sweeps
  • LSK most of the times blocked
  • Throw breaks
  • Backdash intentionally ✅
  • Don't Panic RA
 Intentional input
  • Mashing on knockdowns ➔ learn how to get up
    • Wake up backwards: b
    • Spring kick: 3+4
    • Side Ukemi Right (during fall): 3
    • Side Ukemi Left (during fall): 4
 Basic frame trap
  • Objective FTs (Replace f,df,4,4 with EWGF)
    • 1,2,2 or db,4 to df,2 to f,df,4,4
  • Mental FTs
    • Practice the Back Fist frame trap.
 Replays
  • SST to f,1+2 / EWGS
  • When low HP, think low parry!
