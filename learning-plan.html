Goals for Green, Yellow, and Orange Ranks

Goal Progress
Main character
 Kazuya ✅
Movement comfort
  1. Launcher(s) & follow up: f,n,df,2 or df,1,2 or f,F,3 to df,3 + df,1,2 + f,n,df,3 ➔ practice launchers
  2. Distance issues with WGF and Hell Sweeps ➔ practice before ranked
  3. LSK most of the times blocked ➔ timing issue? Watch replays to see what's wrong
  4. Almost never throw break ➔ learn how to
  5. Backdash rarely used ➔ try to think about it in game
  6. Panic RA which misses 99% ➔ don't use it just when low hp
Intentional input
  1. Mashing on knockdowns ➔ learn how to get up
    • Wake up backwards: b
    • Spring kick: 3+4
    • Side Ukemi Right (during fall): 3
    • Side Ukemi Left (during fall): 4
Basic frame trap
  1. Objective FTs (Replace f,df,4,4 with EWGF)
    • 1,2,2 or db,4 to df,2 to f,df,4,4
  2. Mental FTs
    • 1,2 | 1,2,2 | 1,2,4 | 1,2,2,3 | 1,2,4,3
    • u,4 | u,4,4 | u,4,4,4 | u,4,4,4,4
Replays
  1. SST to f,1+2 / EWGS
  2. When low HP, think low parry!
