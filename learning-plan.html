General Rank-Based Goals

Green / Yellow / Orange
  • Choose main character, learn what buttons do
  • Movement comfort
  • Intentional input, i.e. no mashing
  • One basic frame trap
  • Watch replays to pick up moves
 Red
  • Frame-based movements
  • Punishments
  • Heat optimization
  • Sidestepping, Korean Back Dash, Wave Dash
 Purple
  • Matchup knowledge
  • Replay analysis
  • Combo optimization
  • Duck and punish highs in strings
  • Throw breaks
 Blue
  • Recognize opponent's patterns
  • In-depth matchup knowledge
  • Timing variation
 Tekken King+
  • Address individual weaknesses

Green, Yellow, and Orange

Movement comfort
  • Launcher(s) ➔ drills
    • f,n,df,2
    • f,F,3
  • Follow Up ➔ drills
    • df,3 ➔ df,1,2 ➔ f,n,df,3
  • Distance issues ➔ drills
  • Backdash intentionally ✅
  • Don't Panic RA ✅
 Intentional input
  • Mashing on knockdowns ✅
  • Learn how to get up:
    • Wake up backwards: b
    • Spring kick: 3+4
    • Side Ukemi Right (during fall): 3
    • Side Ukemi Left (during fall): 4
 Basic frame trap
  • Objective FTs
    • 1,2,2/db,4 ➔ df,2 ➔ f,df,4,4
    • Replace f,df,4,4 with EWGF when able
  • Mental FTs
    • Practice the Back Fist frame trap.
 Replays
  • SST to f,1+2 / EWGS
  • When low HP, think low parry!
 Drills
  • Attack drill
  • Block punishment ➔ Level 1 & 2

Red

Movement comfort
  • Launcher(s) + follow up ➔ drills
    • f,n,df,2
    • f,F,3
    • Level 2 (54 to 63 dmg)
    • KBD drill
 Punishments
  • Whiff Punishment
    • Backdash while keeping punishment distance
    • Level 3
  • Block Punishment
    • Level 2
    • Adapt drill to focus on punishing blocked lows:
      1. A1 ➔ Shunkei (M24,-12oB,M), A2 ➔ db,4 (-11oB,L)
      2. A1 ➔ Block, A2 ➔ punish with ws,4,4
 Throw Break
  • Focus on just reacting with drills
    • Jun's Level 1
    • Dragunov's Level 1 & 2
