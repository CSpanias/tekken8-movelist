Green to Red

Basics

  1. Learn one or two launchers & one follow up combo: f,n,df,2 / f,F,3 ➔ df,3 ➔ df,1,2 ➔ f,n,df,3 ✅
  2. Practice block punishment ✅
  3. Backdash intentionally
  4. Use frametraps: Back Fist's four variations (mental frametrap), Back Fist ➔ Abolishing Fist ➔ Elusive Demon (objective frametrap)

Bad Habits

  1. Don't Panic RA ✅
  2. Don't mash on knockdowns ➔ learn how to wake up: b
    3. (backwards), 3+4 (spring kick), 3 (side ukemi right, must be pressed during the fall), 4 (side ukemi left, must be pressed during the fall)✅

Good Habits

  1. Watch high-ranked replays: SST to f,1+2 / EWGS, when low HP ➔ think low parry! ✅
