Green to Red

Basics

  1. Learn one or two launchers & one follow up combo (Level 0): f,n,df,2 / f,F,3 ➔ df,3 ➔ df,1,2 ➔ f,n,df,3 ✅
  2. Practice block punishment ✅
  3. Backdash intentionally
  4. Use frametraps: Back Fist's four variations (mental frametrap), Back Fist ➔ Abolishing Fist ➔ Elusive Demon (objective frametrap)

Bad Habits

  1. Don't Panic RA ✅
  2. Don't mash on knockdowns ➔ learn how to wake up:
    • 1 or 2 (tech roll, away from the screen, must be pressed during the fall)
    • 2 or 3 (tech roll, towards the screen, must be pressed during the fall)
    • 1 (side roll away from the screen)
    • 2 or d,1 (side roll towrads the screen)
    • D,1 (extended side roll, towards the screen)
    • 1,n,D (extended side roll, away from the screen)
    • b (backwards)
    • u (stand quickly)
    • db (stand in crouch)
    • d,3 or d,4 (toe kick, fastest attack option, leads to crouch so ws attack)
    • f,1+2 (forward roll)
    • 3+4 (spring kick)
    • 3 (low get up kick)
    • 4 (mid get up kick)

Good Habits

  1. Watch high-ranked replays: SST to f,1+2 / EWGS, when low HP ➔ think low parry! ✅

    2. Red to Purple

    More Damage

    1. Better follow up (Level 2): f,n,df,2 ➔ b,4 ➔ df,1,DF,2 ➔ dash ➔ b,2,2,1+2 (buffer the 1+2) ✅
    2. Leverage Heat better than just Heat Smash:
      • Devil Impact to WGS: db,3+4,DF,2
      • Abyss Damnation: f,n,df,4,1,1
      • Devil Bleak: 1,1,2,F
      • Tempest Blaster: f,n,df,3

    More Tekken

    1. Learn KBD
    2. Learn Wave Dash
    3. Utilize frametraps: Stature Smash ➔ Abolishing Fist x2, 1,2,4,3 ➔ Soul Thrust
