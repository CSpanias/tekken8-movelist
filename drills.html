Attack

Flash Punch (1,1,2) connection:

  1. Set CPU on Random Standing Block
  2. Go for 2 only when 1,1 connects
  3. Tip: Buffer the second 1 and be ready for 2 by placing your thumb upon it

Defence

Throws

Tip: Try looking at the shoulders and overall character profile rather than zeroing in on the hands!

  1. Pick any character and record three actions: A1 & A2 ➔ mid/high strings, A3 ➔ generic throw
  2. Goal: A1 & A2 ➔ just block, A3 ➔ break

Resources: Stop getting GRIPPED UP! How to Train Against Throws - TEKKEN 8 for Dummies.

  1. Pick Dragunov and record three actions: A1 ➔ f,1+4 (move 106), A2 ➔ f,2+3 (move 107), A3 ➔ uf,1+2 (move 108)
  2. Active A1 only, backdash out of reach and observe Dragunov's hands animation. Repeat for the two others.
  3. Activate A1 with Action Intervals: Shuffle and try to break it five times. Repeat for the two others.
  4. Re-record the actions: A1 ➔ df,1 to f,1+4, A2 ➔ df1 to f,2+3, A3 ➔ df1 to uf,1+2
  5. Goal (for all levels): Break 8/10 throws, both from P1 and P2 side
  6. Level 1:
    • Activate throws in pairs: A1+A2, A1+A3, A2+A3, Action Interval: Normal
  7. Level 2:
    • Activate all three actions
  8. Level 3:
    • Same as Level 2, but set Action Intervals: Shuffle
  9. Level 4:
    • Remove the df1 part and keep just the throws, activate all three actions, Action Intervals: Normal
  10. Level 4:
    • Same as Level 4, but set Action Intervals: Random

Resources: Five Steps to Never Get Grabbed Again - Tekken 8.

Punishments

Block Punishment

WS Punishments:

  1. Set Bryan's Action 1 ➔ df,3 (-26oB), Action 2 ➔ b,1 (-4oB)
  2. Goal: A1 ➔ WS Punishment, A2 ➔ just block

Mixed Punishments:

  1. Set Reina's A1: 1,1,2 (-17oB), A2: db,2 (-16oB), A3: df,2 (-12oB)
  2. Goal: A1 ➔ Wind God Fist + combo, A2 ➔ WS Punishment or low parry, A3 ➔ Flash Punch or Back Fist

Whiff Punishment

  1. Pick Jun as opponent.
  2. Practice settings → Training Mode: Defense
  3. Level 1:
    • Practice settings → Action Intervals: Normal
    • A1 → df,3
    • Goal: Make space for Jun to whiff and launch punish. Focus on the range of your whiff punish.
  4. Level 2:
    • Practice settings → Action Intervals: Shuffle
    • A2 → df,2 (disable A1)
    • Goal: → Make space for Jun to whiff just outside of the hit's range and launch punish. Focus on the range of your whiff punish.
  5. Level 3:
    • Practice settings → Action Intervals: Short
    • A1 → forward dash,df,3, A2 → df,2
    • Goal: A1 → Block, A2 → Whiff punish.
    • Tip: Backdash continously while keeping the right space to whiff punish.
  6. Level 4:
    • Practice settings → Action Intervals: Short
    • A1 → forward dash,df,3, A2 → df,2, A3 → df,4
    • Goal: A1 → Block, A2 → Whiff punish, A3 → Block punish.
    • Tip: Backdash continously while keeping the right space to whiff punish.
  7. If the punishes are proper, the Punish word will turn yellow on the Attack Display information.
