Attack

Flash Punch (1,1,2) connection:

  1. Set CPU on Random Standing Block
  2. Press 2 only when 1,1 connects
    3. for 3' on each side
  3. It's better to be correct and slow, i.e., press late/completely miss 2 when 1,1 connects, rather that wrong and fast, i.e., press 2 when 1,1 doesn't connect
  4. Tip: Buffer the second 1 and be ready for 2 by placing your thumb upon it

Defence

Lows

Lows < 20f are considered unseeable and lows ≥ 20f are considered seeable!

    Level 1: ✅
    1. Bryan (the slowest snake edge of the game): A1 ➔ df,3 (M40,29f,-26oB), A2 ➔ b,1 (M51,20f,+4oB)
    2. A1 ➔ block & punish, A2 ➔ block. Focus on animation as both movements have identical sound.
    Level 2:
    1. Dragunov (slightly faster snake edge vs. Bryan): A1 ➔ df,3 (M48,27f,-26oB), A2 ➔ db,3,1+2 (M49,19f)
    2. A2 is a snake edge variation which fakes it and ends in a grab. The moment the movement animation starts, duck and if grab comes punish.
    Level 3:
    • Bryan: A1 ➔ db,4 (low,12f,-15oB), A2 ➔ df,3 (M40,low,29f,-26oB), A3 ➔ d,df,f,3 (M101,low,19f,-13oB)
    • A1 ➔ block, A2 ➔ ws,2, A3 ➔ ws,1,2
    Level 4:
    • Bryan: A1 ➔ 1,n,db,3 (low,16f,-12oB), A2 ➔ 1,n,df,3 (low,29f,-26oB), A3 ➔ 1,n,uf,4 (mid,-5oB), A4 ➔ 1,n,f,4,1 (mid,mid,18f,-13oB,forces crouch position)
    • A1 ➔ take it, A2 ➔ ws,2, A3 ➔ stand block, A4 ➔ ws,4,4

Throws

Tip: Try looking at the shoulders and overall character profile rather than zeroing in on the hands!

Level 1: ✅

  1. Dragunov: A1 ➔ f,1+4 (M106,1 break), A2 ➔ f,2+3 (M107,2 break), A3 ➔ uf,1+2 (M108,1+2 break)
  2. Activate each action individually and just observe his hands animation
  3. Activate each action individually with Action Intervals: Shuffle and break it 10-20 times
Level 2:
  1. Dragunov: A1 ➔ df,1 to f,1+4, A2 ➔ df,1 to f,2+3, A3 ➔ df,1 to uf,1+2
  2. Activate throws in pairs: A1+A2, A1+A3, A2+A3 with Action Intervals: Normal
  3. Break 8/10 throws, both from P1 and P2 side
Level 3:
  1. Dragunov: Remove the df1 part and keep just the throws
  2. Activate all three with Action Intervals: Shuffle

Mixed

Level 1:
  • Dragunov: A1 ➔ db,3 (M43,mid,17f,-6oB), A2 ➔ db,3,1+2, A3 & A4 & A5 ➔ throws, A6 ➔ b,4,3, A7 ➔ d,2
  • A1 ➔ block & punish, A2 ➔ fuzzy duck & punish, A3 & A4 & A5 ➔ break, A6 ➔ duck & punish, A7 ➔ Low parry
Level 2:
  1. Law: A1 ➔ db,4(26f), A2 ➔ uf,4 (M62,15f), A3 ➔ uf,n,4 (M64,19f), A4 ➔ dss,f,2 (12f), A5 ➔ uf,1,3 (M60,12f), A6 ➔ b,1,2,2 (M48,14f)
  2. A1 ➔ block & launch, A2 & A3➔ standing block & punish, A4 ➔ duck & launch, A5 ➔ block first & low parry second, A6 ➔ block everything & punish

Whiff Punishment

Important: If the Punish word on the Attack Display information doesn't turn yellow, it's not a punish!

Level 1:

  • Dragunov: A1 ➔ b,3 (M53,14f), A2 ➔ b,1,2 (M51,17f,-12oB)
  • A1 ➔ make space to whiff & punish with f,n,df,2, A2 ➔ block & punish with b,1,2
