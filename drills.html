Attack

Flash Punch (1,1,2) connection:

  1. Set CPU on Random Standing Block
  2. Press 2 only when 1,1 connects
    3. for 3' on each side
  3. It's better to be correct and slow, i.e., press late/completely miss 2 when 1,1 connects, rather that wrong and fast, i.e., press 2 when 1,1 doesn't connect
  4. Tip: Buffer the second 1 and be ready for 2 by placing your thumb upon it

Defence

Lows

Lows < 20f are considered unseeable and lows ≥ 20f are considered seeable!

✅ Level 1 (Bryan):

  1. A1 is the slowest snake edge of the game. A2 has an identical sound with A1. Focus on animation rather than the sound.
  2. A1: df,3 (M40,29f,-26oB) ➔ ws,2 (pick the strongest WS launcher)
  3. A2: b,1 (M51,20f,+4oB) ➔ Block

Level 2 (Dragunov):

  1. A1 is a slightly faster snake edge vs. Bryan. A2 is a snake edge variation which fakes it and ends in a grab. The moment the movement animation starts, duck and if grab comes punish.
  2. A1: df,3 (M48,27f,-26oB) ➔ ws,2 (pick the strongest WS launcher)
  3. A2: db,3,1+2 (M49,19f) ➔ ws,2

Level 3 (Bryan):

  1. Start in a crouching position
  2. A1: db,4 (low,12f,-15oB) ➔ Block
  3. A2: df,3 (M40,low,29f,-26oB) ➔ ws,2 (pick the strongest WS launcher)
  4. A3: d,df,f,3 (M101,low,19f,-13oB) ➔ ws,1,2 (pick a 12-13f WS launcher)

Level 4 (Bryan):

  • A1: 1,n,db,3 (low,16f,-12oB) ➔ Take it
  • A2: 1,n,df,3 (low,29f,-26oB) ➔ ws,2 (pick the strongest WS launcher)
  • A3: 1,n,uf,4 (mid,24f,-5oB) ➔ Stand Block
  • A4: 1,n,f,4,1 (mid,mid,18f,-13oB,forces crouch) ➔ ws,4,4 (pick a 12-13f WS launcher)

Throws

Tip: Try looking at the shoulders and overall character profile rather than zeroing in on the hands!

✅ Level 1 (Dragunov):

  1. A1: f,1+4 (M106) ➔ 1 break
  2. A2: f,2+3 (M107) ➔ 2 break
  3. A3: uf,1+2 (M108) ➔ 1+2 break
  4. Activate each action individually and just observe his hands animation
  5. Activate each action individually with Action Intervals: Shuffle and break it 10-20 times

Level 2 (Dragunov):

  1. A1: df,1 to f,1+4 ➔ 1 break
  2. A2: df,1 to f,2+3 ➔ 2 break
  3. A3: df,1 to uf,1+2 ➔ 1+2 break
  4. Activate throws in pairs: A1+A2, A1+A3, A2+A3 with Action Intervals: Normal
  5. Break 8/10 throws, both from P1 and P2 side

Level 3 (Dragunov):

  1. Same as Level 2, but remove the df1 part
  2. Activate all three throws with Action Intervals: Shuffle

Whiff Punishment

If the Punish word on the Attack Display information doesn't turn yellow, it's not a punish!

Level 1 (Dragunov):

  1. A1: b,3 (M53,14f) ➔ Make Space to Whiff & Punish with f,n,df,2
  2. A2: b,1,2 (M51,17f,-12oB) ➔ Block & Punish with b,1,2
  3. Tip: Set Action Intervals to Slow, start in face of Dragunov, and backdash x3 to make him whiff A1
  4. Extra challenges::
    • A1 ➔ Whiff punish with wave dash
    • A2 ➔ Counterhit it with Abolishing Fist or Wind God Step
    • Create space using KBD
