About This Project

This project was created as a way of documenting my journey of learning the Kazuya character in Tekken 8. Its goal is to create a resource that helps me (and hopefully others) understand Kazuya's moves, combos, and strategies. If you're also learning Kazuya or Tekken 8, feel free to explore the pages and use the information provided. Feedback and suggestions are always welcome!
